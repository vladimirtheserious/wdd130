HOME

The basic idea of my site is very simple. Skate parks all over the world are gaining huge popularity. Every second, and maybe even third person in the world would like to ride a bike or roller in a specially equipped place for this. Unfortunately, the skatepark is not accessible to everyone. Not every district, city or country has a skatepark. After all, to build a really good and safe skatepark is not an easy idea. It requires huge expenditures, sufficient materials and, above all, skilled hands. Thats why this project and my site was created. I work with my team to build safe skate parks. The uniqueness of the project lies in the fact that we build skateparks for both rich skateparks communities and low-income ones. Our skateparks are characterized by excellent quality of the figures. The figures themselves are designed with care so that the skatepark is as safe as possible, including for children.
